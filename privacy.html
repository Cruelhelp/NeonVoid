Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

NEXUS VOID collects minimal information to provide the best gaming experience:

  • Username and display name
  • Game statistics and leaderboard scores
  • Ship customization preferences
  • Multiplayer match data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Maintaining leaderboards and rankings
  • Matchmaking in multiplayer modes
  • Saving your progress and customizations
  • Improving game performance and features

3. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect or store personal information on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

NEXUS VOID does not use third-party tracking or analytics services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your game data at any time
  • Request deletion of leaderboard entries
  • Opt out of multiplayer features
